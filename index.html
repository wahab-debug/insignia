Insignia Resort

Welcome to Insignia Resort, located in the beautiful town of Bhurban, Murree, Pakistan. Our resort combines luxury with nature, offering you a peaceful and comfortable stay with stunning views of the hills. Whether you want to relax or explore, Insignia Resort is the perfect place for an unforgettable experience.
