Accommodation for Up to 27 Guests

At Insignia Resort, we offer spacious accommodations that can comfortably host up to 27 guests. Whether you're planning a family reunion, a corporate retreat, or a getaway with friends, our resort provides the perfect setting for your stay.

10 Luxurious Rooms

Our resort features 10 beautifully appointed rooms, each designed to provide the utmost in luxury and comfort. From Deluxe Rooms to spacious apartments, every room is equipped with modern amenities and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. Enjoy a restful night's sleep in a serene and elegant environment.

4 Elegant Lounge Areas

We have four elegant lounge areas where you can unwind, socialize, or simply enjoy a quiet moment. These spaces are perfect for gathering with friends and family, reading a book, or having a casual chat over a cup of tea. Each lounge is designed to offer a cozy yet sophisticated atmosphere.

Scenic Sunroom

Our sunroom is a unique and inviting space that allows you to take in the beauty of Bhurban while staying comfortably indoors. Surrounded by panoramic windows, the sunroom offers stunning views and a bright, cheerful environment. It’s an ideal spot for morning coffee, afternoon tea, or quiet reflection.

3.5 Kanals of Spacious Lawn

Step outside and enjoy 3.5 kanals of lush, green lawn exclusively for our guests. Perfect for picnics, outdoor games, or a leisurely stroll, our spacious lawn offers a tranquil escape where you can connect with nature. It’s your personal sanctuary of peace and relaxation at Insignia Resort.

Ala Carte Breakfast

Start your day with a delightful ala carte breakfast, tailored to your tastes and preferences. Whether you prefer to dine in the lawn, the dining area, or the sunroom, we ensure a personalized dining experience that caters to your desires.
